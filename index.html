Crystals Collector


The computer will choose a random number at the beginning of the game. There are four crystals. By clicking a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the computer generated number. You will lose if your total score goas above the computer number .The value of each crystal is hidden from you, until you click it. The crystals will change value each time the game starts.
